Qualifications & Courses


Education

Junior Developer (Enspiral Dev Academy) - 2015

Enspiral Dev Academy is a bootcamp style computer programming course where over one thousand hours was spent learning hard skills in Ruby and Rails, Javascript and Jquery, Agile, HTML and CSS, Git, and TDD. Communication and Engineering Empathy were the key focus areas for soft skills, and help to integrate students into a realistic working environment, learning how to work in teams and deal with issues that might arise when working with others.

The course had a focus on learning through doing, with ninety five percent of student time spent on hands on programming, and the further five percent in lectures pertinent to the days learning. This took place over an eighteen week period and was divided into four phases, with each phase building on the knowledge gained in the last. Phase zero was a part time remote working phase focusing on core skills and syntax familiarity needed during the full immersion part of the course. Phases one through three then took students from pairing and building basic terminal apps, to working with web frame works such as azure and rails, culminating in a full team final project which traverses multiple languages in order to build a market ready product.
